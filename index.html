About Us

Propertyhill Realty (Pvt) Ltd is a registered real estate company established in October 2013. We provide professional services in all property-related matters including valuations, sales, auctions, property management, and development consultancy. We are duly registered with the Estate Agents Council of Zimbabwe and the Valuers Council of Zimbabwe.
