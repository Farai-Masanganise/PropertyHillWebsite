About Propertyhill Realty

Propertyhill Realty (Pvt) Ltd is a registered real estate company established in October 2013. We specialize in valuations, property sales, auctions, management, and development consultancy.

Registered with the Estate Agents Council of Zimbabwe and the Valuers Council of Zimbabwe, we deliver transparent, professional, and efficient real estate solutions across Zimbabwe.

We are a trusted real estate agency in Zimbabwe offering property valuation, sales, leasing, and management services. Our team combines industry expertise with a client-first approach to ensure smooth and reliable property transactions.
