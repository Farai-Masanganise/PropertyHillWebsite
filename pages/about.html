About Propertyhill Realty

Innovative Solutions

Propertyhill Realty (Pvt) Ltd is a professional real estate consultancy established in October 2013. Since inception, we have provided reliable and professional property services throughout Zimbabwe, helping individuals, businesses, financial institutions, developers, and government entities make informed property decisions.

Built on integrity, professionalism, and market expertise, Propertyhill Realty has grown into a respected name within Zimbabwe's real estate industry, offering trusted advice and dependable property solutions.

Our Story

Propertyhill Realty was founded with a clear vision: to provide professional property services backed by technical expertise, transparency, and exceptional client service.

What began as a small real estate practice has developed into a multi-disciplinary property consultancy serving a diverse client base across Zimbabwe. Over the years, we have successfully handled property valuations, sales, leasing assignments, and consultancy projects in both urban and emerging markets.

Our continued growth reflects our commitment to maintaining high professional standards while adapting to the changing needs of Zimbabwe's property sector.
